UAV Industry Integration

SafetyDrone is actively building an ecosystem of trusted partners so public safety agencies can run modern, integrated drone operations without extra manual work. Our platform connects with leading UAV hardware and software vendors to streamline everything from flight log imports to compliance reporting and maintenance tracking. Using our drone log import tools, APIs, and web services, you can pull mission data directly from your aircraft and flight apps into SafetyDrone—no spreadsheets required.

As the drone industry evolves—with new aircraft, sensors, and regulations—SafetyDrone evolves with it. We continually expand our list of supported partners and refine key features so first responders, emergency management, and other public safety teams always have a workflow that matches the latest technology and operational requirements. The drone industry isn’t standing still, and neither should your data and compliance tools. Safety Drone

Our Key Partners

Skydio
Skydio is a U.S.-based drone manufacturer and a recognized leader in autonomous flight technology. Their aircraft combine advanced AI with robust airframes to support demanding public safety, enterprise, and government missions. SafetyDrone integrates with Skydio platforms so flight data and mission records flow directly into your UAS program logs. Safety Drone
Autel Robotics
Autel Robotics develops versatile aerial robotics systems used by public safety agencies, government users, and commercial operators around the world. Backed by a global footprint across North America, Europe, and Asia, Autel delivers flexible platforms for both day-to-day and specialized missions. SafetyDrone integration helps public safety customers automatically capture and manage Autel flight logs for training, operations, and compliance. Safety Drone
Wingtra
Wingtra, based in Zurich, Switzerland, manufactures the WingtraOne VTOL drone designed for high-precision mapping and surveying. Its fixed-wing performance and vertical takeoff and landing capabilities make it ideal for large-area data collection in challenging environments. Through our integration, SafetyDrone can ingest Wingtra flight data so agencies and partners can maintain a complete operational history alongside other aircraft in their fleet. Safety Drone

SafetyDrone Mobile Apps

The SafetyDrone solution is based on a cloud infrastructure with secure data storage, online web application accompanied by our mobile application for offline access and easy synchronization with your cloud-based account. In addition, the mobile app provides you with access to the latest airspace status updates, local weather and solar index readings that might affect your operations.


Another Android mobile application, DLBSync, simplifies import of your flights from major mobile drone flight control apps natively to your SafetyDrone account. This app can sync flights into DLB Sync from your flight control apps when offline or in poor mobile coverage, then upload flights to SafetyDrone account when you have mobile or Wi-Fi coverage.
