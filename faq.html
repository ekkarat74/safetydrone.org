SafetyDrone FAQ

The major points of our drone logbook are answered in one page. See what makes SafetyDrone the industry leader.

Data Storage Privacy and Security
Where/how is my data stored?
All of your data is stored on secure cloud servers and backed up daily.
What do you do with my data?
We do not sell or distribute your personal data (be it contact information (email), UAV telemetry or other drone and flight data without your permission. We do however look at the types of drones in our system at an aggregate level so that we can better serve our customers with applications for specific drones.
Product Support
How can I upgrade my SafetyDrone plan?
To upgrade your SafetyDrone plan you need to first cancel your existing plan (if on a paid plan). Once you have cancelled it we will rebate to you the prorated balance of your cancelled plan. You can then upgrade to a new plan.
What happens to my data when my subscription expires?
When your subscription expires you will lose access to the functionality of the plan you were on. You do NOT lose your data, just access to the plan features. Once you subscribe again you get access back.
How often are new features added?
We are constantly adding features to SafetyDrone. Look at our blog to find out the latest upgrades and new features. You can track these updates from our release notes.
Can I contact you with ideas to improve the service?
You, the drone pilot provide real world experience to help us improve the logbook functionality and workflow so we always welcome your feedback. Click the “Contact Us” tab to send us a note.
What drones / software do you support?
We support log files and telemetry from over 50 suppliers including DJI, Parrot, Autel, Wingtra, Yuneec, Wingcopter, SenseFly, Flyability, Vantage Robotics, 3DR (Pixhawk), DroneDeploy, Pix4d, DroneLink, DroneHarmony, Autopilot (Auto Flight Logic) and many more. SafetyDrone Mobile Apps

The SafetyDrone solution is based on a cloud infrastructure with secure data storage, online web application accompanied by our mobile application for offline access and easy synchronization with your cloud based account. In addition, the mobile app provides you with access to the latest airspace status updates, local weather and solar index reading that might affect your operations.


Another Android mobile application, DLBSync, simplifies import of your flights from major mobile drone flight control apps natively to your SafetyDrone account. This app can sync flights into DLB Sync from your flight control apps when offline or in poor mobile coverage, then upload flights to SafetyDrone account when you have mobile or WIFI coverage.
