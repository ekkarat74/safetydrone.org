SafetyDrone Advantage

Built for Public Safety Drone Operations

SafetyDrone is purpose-built to automate flight logging for police, fire, EMS, SAR, and emergency management UAS teams. The platform supports more telemetry file types than any other public safety–focused logbook, so you can capture missions from mixed fleets and different flight apps in one place—regardless of manufacturer or software vendor. As your program adds new aircraft, payloads, and controllers, SafetyDrone scales with you, keeping incident, training, and exercise records consistent and easy to retrieve for audits, grants, and public records requests.

Main Features

Log your flights with detailed information

Import mission logs to auto-fill flight details, map the GPS path, and replay incidents or training flights in 3D for debrief. SafetyDrone accepts logs from all major drone manufacturers used in public safety—including DJI, Parrot, Autel, Wingcopter, Flyability, Wingtra, senseFly, Yuneec, and more—covering 80+ different log formats so your entire fleet is covered.
More than 80 log types can be imported.

Generate custom and compliance-ready reports

Create PDF reports branded with your agency crest and contact details. Generate regulator-ready reports (FAA, CAA, CASA, CAD, EASA, DGAC, TBST) in seconds when command staff, city leadership, or oversight bodies request documentation of your UAS activity for a specific case, date range, or incident type.

Store all organization & flight documents

Keep all mission and program documents in one secure system: pre-flight and post-flight forms, checklists, risk assessments, COAs, waivers, SOPs, and training records. Build custom checklists and risk-assessment templates that match your department’s policies and attach them directly to operations for a complete case history.

Manage drone inspections and maintenance

Set up inspection and maintenance plans for every aircraft and payload in your fleet. SafetyDrone notifies you when inspections, firmware checks, or maintenance are coming due, helping you prove airworthiness and show that equipment used on incidents is being maintained according to manufacturer guidance and agency policy.

Plan Missions

Plan missions around events and emergency responses by checking controlled airspace, local restrictions, and temporary flight rules. Attach risk assessments, checklists, and mission paperwork so each flight—whether planned or rapid response—has the documentation needed for post-incident review.

Built for “show up and fly” operations

Public safety missions are often time-critical, where seconds matter more than paperwork. SafetyDrone is designed so pilots can show up and launch quickly while the rest of the team manages the admin. From a mobile phone or tablet, supervisors or support staff can handle checklists, risk forms, and mission details while the pilot focuses on getting the aircraft in the air. If there’s no time to set up a mission beforehand, crews can create or complete missions after the fact, attach logs and forms, and still maintain a clean, auditable record—without slowing down life-saving operations at the scene.

Manage your organization and pilots

Monitor pilot currency and experience for both operations and training. Store and track pilot and agency documents—licenses, medicals, training certificates, policies—and share key updates across the organization. Use advanced personnel profiling to track skills and capabilities by regulation, mission type, platform, or role, with both predefined and custom skill sets.

Manage your equipment

Track hours and usage for cameras, thermal sensors, spotlights, speakers, and other mission-critical equipment. Record maintenance actions and service intervals to reduce unexpected failures during calls and extend the life of expensive gear.

Manage battery cycles

Log battery charging cycles and assign packs to specific aircraft or missions. Monitor performance trends over time so you can retire aging or under-performing batteries before they become a safety risk and ensure that only healthy packs are deployed to incidents.

Report your flight incidents

Record UAS-related incidents and anomalies with full context: pilots, observers, aircraft, batteries, payloads, and supporting documents. Share incident reports with your safety or compliance officer and maintain a clear, auditable record for internal review, training, and external investigations.

SafetyDrone Mobile Apps

The SafetyDrone platform is built on secure cloud infrastructure with a modern web application and companion mobile apps. Crews can access mission data from the station, the EOC, or the field, work offline when coverage is poor, and sync back to the cloud when connectivity returns. The mobile app also provides up-to-date airspace status, local weather, and solar-activity readings that may affect flight safety.

An additional Android app, DLBSync, streamlines importing flights from major mobile drone flight-control apps directly into your SafetyDrone account. It can capture logs even during low-coverage operations and upload them later over Wi-Fi or mobile data, reducing the risk of missing or incomplete mission records.


Another Android mobile application, DLBSync, simplifies import of your flights from major mobile drone flight control apps natively to your SafetyDrone account. This app can sync flights into DLB Sync from your flight control apps when offline or in poor mobile coverage, then upload flights to SafetyDrone account when you have mobile or Wi-Fi coverage.
